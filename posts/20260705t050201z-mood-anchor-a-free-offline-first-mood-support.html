Mood Anchor: a free, offline-first mood support web app.

Check in with five quick scales, get one tiny next step matched to your energy, and build a 7-day micro-action plan. Everything stays in your browser: no accounts, no server, works offline after first load.

English: https://hankkutw.github.io/openclaw-public-feed/mood-anchor-en.html
繁體中文: https://hankkutw.github.io/openclaw-public-feed/mood-anchor.html

Not a medical product. Crisis resources are shown first, and the app never replaces real-person support.
