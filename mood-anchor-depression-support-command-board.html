NICE NG222

Evidence: Guided self-help may use digital CBT, behavioural activation, problem-solving, or psychoeducation material with practitioner support.

Team rule: Keep Mood Anchor as local self-support and product QA only; do not present guided self-help as something this PWA provides without practitioner involvement.

NIMH

Evidence: Self-care can support treatment and recovery, and people should seek professional help for severe or distressing symptoms lasting two weeks or more.

Team rule: Include professional help routing in the safe-start and low-mood flows; do not frame owner-entered signals as clinical progress.

NHS

Evidence: Exercise and self-help materials can help some people cope, and people who feel down for a while or not like themselves should see a GP.

Team rule: Tiny activity experiments must stay optional, small, and low-pressure; include a GP/professional-help prompt when low mood persists or feels unusual.

NHS inform

Evidence: The self-help guide is CBT-based, keeps privacy by not requiring login, and is not intended for crisis use.

Team rule: Keep Mood Anchor no-login, local-first, and explicit that crisis moments require dedicated crisis or emergency support.

988 Lifeline

Evidence: 988 provides call, text, and chat crisis support in the United States.

Team rule: Crisis-first routing overrides all personalization; if crisis or self-harm confusion appears, pause distribution and strengthen emergency-resource copy.

CDC

Evidence: Social connection and support can help people manage stress, anxiety, and depression.

Team rule: Trusted-person scripts may support connection, but they remain copy-only drafts; no contact access, auto-send, or automated outreach.

部門任務

Product Studio QA

Verify Mood Anchor is a private, responsive, offline-capable support tool with personalization that stays local.

  • Run through the seven feature lanes on mobile and desktop, including offline reload after first visit.
  • Confirm action ladder, low-mood card, activity experiment board, support scripts, and care cards use localStorage or static copy only.
  • Check crisis-first routing, professional-help prompts, reset/export controls, service worker, manifest, and stable layout before any wider distribution.

Support/Ops

Enforce safety boundaries before public distribution.

  • Pause if crisis, self-harm, emergency, treatment, therapy, diagnosis, monitoring, or medical-advice confusion appears.
  • Check all user-facing copy against the official evidence anchors and the exact boundaries on this board.
  • Confirm no forms, no mailto links, no auto-send, no contact access, no OpenClaw message control, and no server collection.

Analytics

Use owner-entered aggregate only.

  • Record only aggregate usefulness, privacy-trust, too-hard-step, support-risk, and safety-confusion counts after owner review.
  • Do not collect private mood notes, activity history, personal contacts, medical details, account data, or scraped analytics.
  • Recommend pause, onboarding revision, one owner-reviewed channel pass, or further local QA from decision gates only.

Marketing Desk

Prepare non-medical English positioning without posting.

  • Describe Mood Anchor as a private offline mood-support PWA with crisis resources and trusted-person scripts.
  • Link to the English PWA, care card, support scripts, safe-start checklist, low-mood card, and activity experiment board only after Support/Ops signs off.
  • Do not post, submit, schedule, DM, email, or make revenue, attention, health, or outcome promises.

停止線

  • Mood Anchor is not diagnosis, treatment, therapy, crisis care, or medical advice; it is a local self-support and reflection tool.
  • No server collection: use localStorage, copy controls, export controls, and owner-entered aggregate only.
  • Do not collect private mood notes, activity history, personal contacts, medical details, account data, analytics, or customer messages.
  • Do not promise revenue, attention, productivity, mood, safety, clinical, crisis, or health outcomes.
  • Do not send, post, schedule, email, DM, submit, scrape, auto-reply, contact a trusted person, or use OpenClaw message commands from this board.
  • Crisis or self-harm confusion pauses distribution and routes to emergency/crisis-resource copy review before any other task.
