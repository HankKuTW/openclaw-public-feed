Title

AI Digital Product Terms & License Kit

Subtitle

A manual AI prompt kit for drafting clearer digital product terms, refund boundaries, delivery notes, usage rights, and support scope.

Description

Before you send traffic to a digital product, buyers need to know what they get, how files are delivered, what usage rights are included, and what support is available.

AI Digital Product Terms & License Kit gives you a manual copy-paste workflow for turning your product facts into plain-language terms, refund and replacement boundaries, delivery notes, usage-rights language, and support-scope copy.

Built for Gumroad, Etsy, Ko-fi, Creative Market, and similar digital product sellers who want clearer buyer-facing terms without setting up software or making legal claims.

This is not legal advice, not platform compliance advice, not an automated messaging tool, and it does not promise refund or dispute outcomes. You review every output before using it.
