合格 audit 請求

qualified_audit_request

qualified_audit_request

Owner signal: Owner has a public English listing URL and the request fits listing clarity, offer positioning, proof, pricing presentation, or trust-friction review.

Team action: Revenue Ops maps the request to the qualified-audit reply draft and prepares local owner-review notes only.

Metric: Count qualified requests and note source channel after the owner records it.

缺公開 listing URL

needs_public_listing_url

needs_public_listing_url

Owner signal: Interest exists but the requester did not provide a public visitor-openable listing URL.

Team action: Revenue Ops maps to the missing-URL draft and Marketing Desk tightens future request-pack wording.

Metric: Count missing-URL cases and compare them with qualified requests before another channel pass.

超出服務範圍

out_of_scope

out_of_scope

Owner signal: Request asks for ads, SEO implementation, store setup, legal/account advice, code work, or ongoing operations.

Team action: Revenue Ops maps to the out-of-scope draft and keeps the free grader as the fallback surface.

Metric: Count scope mismatch reasons as aggregate objections only.

需要 owner 複核

owner_review_required

owner_review_required

Owner signal: Scope, timing, platform-rule fit, sensitive data, support risk, or buyer status is unclear.

Team action: Support/Ops pauses reply use until Codex records a local review decision or the owner handles an external-authority item.

Metric: Count owner-review cases separately from qualified demand.

支援或退款問題

support_or_refund_questions

support_or_refund_questions

Owner signal: Message relates to support, refund, delivery, dissatisfaction, platform dispute, account, or payment trouble.

Team action: Support/Ops pauses distribution and blocks sales routing; no refund or support reply is delegated.

Metric: Count support/refund questions as a pause signal, not as leads.

已付款 audit 交付

paid_audit_fulfillment

paid_audit_fulfillment

Owner signal: The owner has confirmed payment outside this board and needs a fulfillment reply draft.

Team action: Revenue Ops prepares only the local fulfillment note; owner edits and sends any real reply manually.

Metric: Count paid-audit confirmations only after owner confirmation.

Owner-only 佇列

  • Owner supplies the public post URL and source channel after any manual public action.
  • Owner records observed replies, qualified audit requests, missing URL counts, objections, support/refund questions, and paid-audit confirmations.
  • Owner confirms any payment, checkout, invoice, refund, delivery timing, or support context outside this board.
  • Only the owner sends or replies manually after editing any draft.

Local only. No server collection.

部門任務

Revenue Ops

Map demand-ledger classifications to owner-reply-handoff scenarios.

  • Use the six validation lanes to choose the copy-only draft that matches the owner-entered classification.
  • Prepare checkout-pending wording only; no checkout creation, invoices, prices, refunds, or customer commitments.
  • Hand off qualified-audit notes to Codex for local review before any owner-only reply or payment path.

Marketing Desk

Prepare channel-safe prompts without posting.

  • Use missing-URL and out-of-scope counts to revise request-pack wording and channel prompts.
  • Do not post, submit, schedule, DM, email, comment, scrape, or auto-publish.
  • Keep drafts pointed at the free grader, request pack, proof pack, and owner-reviewed reply handoff.

Analytics

Use owner-entered aggregate only.

  • Record public post URL, reply counts, qualified requests, missing URLs, support/refund questions, and paid-audit confirmations only after the owner supplies them.
  • Do not scrape analytics, comments, profiles, directories, private listing text, or customer messages.
  • Recommend pause, request-pack wording revision, or owner-reviewed service reply packet from aggregate counts only.

Support/Ops

Apply pause rules before any next channel pass.

  • Pause distribution when support/refund, owner-review, sensitive-data, account, payment, or platform-rule risk appears.
  • Confirm the board remains static, local-only, no-server, and low-load after generation.
  • Block revenue, traffic, conversion, ranking, platform approval, customer acquisition, treatment, or health-outcome promises.
