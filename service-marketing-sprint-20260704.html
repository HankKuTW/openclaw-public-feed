Owner-only actions

  1. Create or choose the service checkout URL manually before using paid CTAs.
  2. Pick three allowed manual surfaces for the first week: one owned profile, one service directory or maker community, and one value-post community.
  3. Review channel rules before posting; if rules are unclear, do not post.
  4. Record inquiries, qualified inquiries, paid audits, upsells, refunds, and support issues daily.
  5. Stop distribution if replies show confusion about scope, claims, turnaround, or refund terms.

Strict stop line

  • Do not auto-post, schedule, email, send unsolicited direct messages, scrape contacts, buy votes, ask for votes, or use external authority.
  • Do not log in, publish, create checkout, change price, payout, tax, refund, account, credential, discount, or platform settings automatically.
  • Do not promise revenue, traffic, conversion, ranking, approval, or customer outcomes as certain.
  • Owner must manually review checkout setup, channel rules, and every external draft before use.
