Official Evidence

NIMH self-care

https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/caring-for-your-mental-health

Small self-care actions and help-seeking can be framed as general support; Mood Anchor should stay personal, optional, and non-clinical.

SAMHSA 988

https://www.samhsa.gov/mental-health/988

988 is the escalation route for crisis or emotional distress; Mood Anchor should point users outward instead of trying to handle crises.

988 Lifeline

https://988lifeline.org/

Call, text, and chat support is available through 988; crisis-routing copy must stay visible.

NICE depression recommendations

https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng222/chapter/recommendations

Structured self-help and activity planning are support concepts; Mood Anchor must not present itself as clinical care.
